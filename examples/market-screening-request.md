# Example: Market Screening Request

```text
Use $amazon-product-manager to screen these Amazon US market ideas for a small brand:

- bamboo coffee filter holder
- coffee capsule drawer organizer
- ceramic matcha whisk stand

Constraints:
- Target price: $15-$35
- Prefer bamboo, ceramic, or light metal
- Avoid high-compliance electronics
- First validation batch should stay under $5,000 landed cost

Output:
- Enter Market / Watch Market / Avoid Market decision for each idea
- Score across demand, competition, profit room, differentiation, operations, and capital pressure
- Best first wedge
- Missing evidence that would change the decision
- Next 7-day validation plan
```
