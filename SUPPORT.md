# Support

This is an open-source skill project, not a managed support service.

## Good Ways To Ask For Help

- Open a documentation issue when a workflow, template, or example is unclear.
- Open a workflow request when you need a new Amazon product-management decision pattern.
- Include the marketplace, product stage, decision needed, and public or anonymized evidence.
- Say what output you expected and what was confusing.

## Please Do Not Include

- Account credentials, cookies, sessions, or browser profiles.
- Raw seller exports, paid-tool datasets, customer data, supplier quotes, or private screenshots.
- Private ASIN-level business performance unless it has been anonymized.
- Requests for legal, tax, medical, or financial advice as a substitute for a qualified professional.

## Maintainer Response

Issues are handled on a best-effort basis. Clear, narrow, anonymized examples are more likely to receive useful feedback.
